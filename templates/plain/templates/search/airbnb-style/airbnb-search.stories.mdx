# Airbnb‑style Search (USWDS)

A static, USWDS‑only recreation of the familiar Airbnb search pattern.

## Includes
- Destination combo-box (autocomplete)
- Start/End date pickers
- Filters modal (type, beds, price)
- Results cards with sort + List/Map toggle

> Open `airbnb-search.html` directly to interact.

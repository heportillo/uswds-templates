Message from Secretary Martinez

Our mission at HHS is to enhance the health and well-being of all Americans. We work every day to provide essential services that protect our most vulnerable citizens and ensure everyone has access to quality healthcare.

From responding to public health emergencies to advancing medical research, from ensuring food safety to supporting families in need, HHS touches the lives of millions of Americans every day. We are committed to transparency, innovation, and evidence-based policies that make a real difference.
